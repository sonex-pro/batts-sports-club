Chen Haotian

Chen Haotian, 21 years old, was born in Tianjin, China.
He started playing table tennis at the age of 6, went to Beijing Shichahai Sports School for training at the age of 8, and returned to Tianjin Team for training at the age of 12, and has been training ever since.
He won the sixth place in the National Youth Championship (U15) team, the tenth place in the National Youth Championship (U15) singles, the fourth place in the National Youth Seedling Training (U15), then third place in the National Youth Championships (U18), and the fifth place in the National Championships twice in a row.
He participated in the national team training three times in a row in 2017, 2018, and 2019.
In the 2019 China Table Tennis League A, he defeated Lin Shidong 3:1 and accumulated many Chinese national team athletes.
He has been a main player of the Tianjin Table Tennis Team for many years.
