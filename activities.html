About Our Centre

Our centre welcomes a variety of activities including pickle ball, badminton, short-mat bowls, short tennis, conferences and parties. Our sports hall spans approximately 22m x 18m, featuring semi-sprung flooring and top-quality sports hall lighting. Additionally, we offer an upstairs viewing gallery overlooking the sports hall, which can be hired for conferences or other small functions.
